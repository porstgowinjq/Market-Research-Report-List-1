<p><strong>The Antiretroviral Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Class Drugs Combination</li><li>NRTI</li><li>NNRTI</li><li>Protease Inhibitors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antiretroviral drug market comprises various categories that target HIV. Multi-class drug combinations leverage multiple mechanisms to enhance efficacy and reduce resistance. NRTIs (Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors) block viral replication by mimicking natural nucleosides. NNRTIs (Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors) attach to the reverse transcriptase enzyme, hindering its function. Protease inhibitors prevent viral maturation by inhibiting the protease enzyme, while other market segments include fusion inhibitors and integrase inhibitors, broadening treatment options for HIV-infected patients.</p></p>
