<p><p>Application Release Orchestration (ARO) software is categorized into two primary market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. They enable teams to deploy updates quickly without substantial infrastructure investment. Conversely, On-Premises ARO solutions are installed locally within an organization's data center, providing greater control over data security and compliance but requiring higher initial costs and maintenance efforts. Both types cater to diverse organizational needs.</p></p>
