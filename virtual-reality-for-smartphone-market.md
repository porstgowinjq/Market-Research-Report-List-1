<p><p>The smartphone virtual reality market can be categorized into two main types: Regular VR and Cardboard VR. Regular VR typically involves dedicated headsets that provide immersive experiences, utilizing advanced technologies for tracking and interactivity. These devices often enhance performance and visual quality for gaming and applications. In contrast, Cardboard VR refers to simple, cost-effective solutions made from cardboard that utilize smartphones as displays.
