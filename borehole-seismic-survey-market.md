<p><strong>The Borehole Seismic Survey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Offset Vertical Seismic Profile</li><li>Zero-Offset Vertical Seismic Profile</li><li>Deviated-well Vertical Seismic Profile</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The borehole seismic survey market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Offset Vertical Seismic Profile (OVSP)**: Measures seismic waves from a distance off the well, providing information about subsurface structures.</p><p>2. **Zero-Offset Vertical Seismic Profile (ZVSP)**: Involves data collection directly above the borehole, offering high-resolution images of the geological formations.</p><p>3. **Deviated-well Vertical Seismic Profile**: Conducted in angled wells, capturing seismic responses for complex geological environments.</p><p>4. **Others**: This category encompasses emerging techniques and variations tailored to specific geological or operational needs.</p></p>
