<p><strong>The PCB Solder Mask Exposure Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Contact Exposure"</li><li>"Direct Imaging"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB solder mask exposure machine market consists of two main types: Contact Exposure and Direct Imaging. Contact Exposure uses a film negative placed in direct contact with the photoresist-coated PCB, allowing UV light to transfer the mask pattern precisely. In contrast, Direct Imaging employs laser or UV sources to project the image directly onto the PCB, eliminating the need for a physical mask. Both methods significantly enhance production efficiency and precision in the PCB manufacturing process.</p></p>
