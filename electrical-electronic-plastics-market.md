<p><p>The Electrical & Electronic Plastics market comprises various types, including PA6, PA66 FR, PBT, and PET, each offering distinct properties for different applications. PA6 (Polyamide 6) is known for its toughness and flexibility, while PA66 FR (Flame Retardant) enhances fire resistance. PBT (Polybutylene Terephthalate) provides excellent dimensional stability and heat resistance, making it suitable for electrical components. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is valued for its chemical resistance and temperature stability, often used in connectors and insulation materials.</p></p>
