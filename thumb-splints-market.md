<p><strong>The Thumb Splints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Abduction</li><li>Stabilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thumb splints market is categorized into two main types: abduction splints and stabilization splints. Abduction splints are designed to position the thumb away from the palm, promoting proper healing after injuries or surgeries while preventing excessive movement. Stabilization splints, on the other hand, focus on immobilizing the thumb to provide support during rehabilitation or for chronic conditions, like arthritis. Both types aim to enhance comfort and functionality, aiding recovery and improving the quality of life for users.</p></p>
