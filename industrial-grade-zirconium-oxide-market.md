<p><p>The industrial grade zirconium oxide market comprises two primary types: fused zirconium oxide and chemical zirconium oxide. Fused zirconium oxide is produced by melting zirconia in an electric furnace, resulting in a high-purity product suitable for applications like abrasives and refractory materials. Chemical zirconium oxide, on the other hand, is derived from chemical processes and is used in various applications including catalysts, ceramics, and electronics due to its unique properties like high resistance to corrosion and excellent thermal stability.</p></p>
