<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The EPS Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EPS motors, or Electric Power Steering motors, are categorized into various types based on their functionality and design. CEPS (Column Electric Power Steering) motors are integrated within the steering column for compactness. REPS (Rack Electric Power Steering) motors are mounted on the rack, providing direct assistance for improved handling. PEPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering) motors offer a balance between performance and weight. EHPS (Electric Hydraulic Power Steering) motors combine electric assistance with hydraulic systems, enhancing steering feel and efficiency. Each type addresses specific vehicle requirements, contributing to performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
