<p><ul><li>SMD Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The current sense resistors market is primarily divided into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and Through Hole types. SMD current sense resistors are compact, easily mounted on printed circuit boards, and suited for automated manufacturing processes, making them ideal for modern electronics. Conversely, Through Hole current sense resistors offer robust mechanical stability and are preferred for applications requiring higher power handling and thermal dissipation. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
