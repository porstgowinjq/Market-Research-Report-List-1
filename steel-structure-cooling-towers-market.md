<p><strong>The Steel Structure Cooling Towers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Open-Circuit Cooling Towers"</li><li>"Closed-Circuit Cooling Towers"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel structure cooling towers market is divided into two main types: open-circuit and closed-circuit cooling towers. Open-circuit cooling towers utilize ambient air to cool water through evaporation, making them efficient for various industrial processes. In contrast, closed-circuit cooling towers maintain a separation between the process water and the cooling media, using a heat exchanger to transfer heat without direct exposure to the environment. This design minimizes contamination and water loss, catering to industries with strict cooling requirements.</p></p>
