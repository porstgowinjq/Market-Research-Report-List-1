<p><strong>The dPCR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Droplet Digital PCR</li><li>Chip-based Digital PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital PCR (dPCR) encompasses two primary market types: Droplet Digital PCR (ddPCR) and Chip-based Digital PCR. ddPCR utilizes micro-droplets to partition DNA samples, enabling precise quantification of nucleic acids by isolating reactions within distinct droplets. In contrast, Chip-based Digital PCR employs specially designed chips to contain and manipulate DNA samples, offering high throughput and reduced reagent usage. Both approaches provide enhanced sensitivity and accuracy for applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and genetic testing, driving advancements in molecular biology.</p></p>
