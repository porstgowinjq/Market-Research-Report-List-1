<p><p>The Automated Urine Analyzers market is primarily categorized into two key types: Chemistry Method and Microscopy Method. The Chemistry Method relies on chemical reactions and spectrophotometry to assess various analytes in urine, providing quantitative results on compounds like proteins, sugars, and pH levels. Conversely, the Microscopy Method involves automated imaging and analysis of urine sediment, identifying elements such as cells, crystals, and bacteria. Both methods enhance diagnostic accuracy and efficiency in clinical laboratories, streamlining urine analysis processes.</p></p>
