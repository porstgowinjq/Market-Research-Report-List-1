<p><p>The robotic case packing system market is primarily segmented into Side Load and Top Load systems. Side Load systems involve robots that access the product from the side of the case, ideal for handling larger or heavier items, enhancing flexibility in packaging. In contrast, Top Load systems allow robots to place products directly from above into the case, making it suitable for lighter, more delicate items. Both types optimize packaging efficiency and speed, catering to different industry requirements.</p></p>
