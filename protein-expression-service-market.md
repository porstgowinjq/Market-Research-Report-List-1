<p><p>The Protein Expression Service market comprises various systems tailored for specific needs. Prokaryotic expression systems, like bacteria, offer cost-effective and rapid protein production, ideal for simpler proteins. Mammalian cell expression systems are used for complex proteins requiring post-translational modifications, ensuring higher similarity to natural proteins. Yeast cell expression systems combine the advantages of prokaryotic and eukaryotic systems, providing robust expression with some modifications. Other markets may include insect cell systems and cell-free systems, catering to diverse protein production requirements.</p></p>
