<p><p>The automatic parking robots market is categorized into three main types: magnetic guided, laser guided, and others. Magnetic guided systems utilize magnetic strips embedded in the ground to direct vehicles during parking, ensuring precise movements. Laser guided systems employ laser sensors to detect obstacles and navigate accurately within parking facilities. Other systems may include vision-based or hybrid technologies, integrating various sensing methods for enhanced efficiency and adaptability in diverse parking environments. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
