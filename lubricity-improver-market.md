<p><strong>The Lubricity Improver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acidic Lubricity Improver</li><li>Non-Acidic Lubricity Improver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lubricity improver market is segmented into two main types: acidic and non-acidic lubricity improvers. Acidic lubricity improvers enhance the lubrication properties of fuels and oils by reducing friction and wear through chemical reactions with metal surfaces. In contrast, non-acidic lubricity improvers primarily function through physical mechanisms, such as forming a protective film on surfaces without altering the chemical composition. Both types serve to enhance the performance and longevity of lubricants in various industrial applications, improving equipment efficiency.</p></p>
