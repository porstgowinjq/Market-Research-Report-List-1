<p><p>Retail e-commerce software is primarily offered in two market types: On-Premise and Software-as-a-Service (SaaS). On-Premise solutions require businesses to install and maintain the software on their own servers, offering more control and customization but demanding higher upfront costs and IT resources. In contrast, SaaS delivers software via the cloud, allowing businesses to access it on a subscription basis, providing scalability and automatic updates with lower initial costs, making it ideal for smaller retailers or those seeking flexibility.</p></p>
