<p><p>The combustion particle analyzer market comprises two primary types: combustion gas particle analyzers and particle combustion analyzers. Combustion gas particle analyzers are designed to measure particulate emissions in exhaust gases, providing insights into air quality and compliance with environmental regulations. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing combustion processes and minimizing emissions in various industries.</p></p>
