<p><strong>The Sorting Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tilt-tray</li><li>Cross-belt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sorting systems in logistics typically utilize two main market types: tilt-tray and cross-belt systems. Tilt-tray sorters use trays that tilt to drop items into chutes, efficiently handling a variety of products. In contrast, cross-belt sorters employ modular belts that transport items and can divert them at high speeds to multiple destinations. Both systems enhance sorting efficiency, accuracy, and speed, making them suitable for various industries, including e-commerce and retail, where rapid order fulfillment is crucial.</p></p>
