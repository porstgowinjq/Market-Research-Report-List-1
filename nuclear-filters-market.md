<p><p>The nuclear filters market consists of three main types: HEPA filters, ULPA filters, and other specialized filters. HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters capture 99.97% of particles 0.3 microns or larger, making them essential in nuclear facilities for air purification. ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters offer even higher efficiency, trapping 99.999% of particles down to 0.1 microns. Other filters include custom solutions designed for specific radioactive environments or applications, ensuring safety and compliance in nuclear settings.</p></p>
