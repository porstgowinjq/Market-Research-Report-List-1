<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CMOS Image Sensor Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Backside-illuminated (BSI)</li><li>BSI Stacked</li><li>Front-illuminated (FI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS image sensor module market is primarily categorized into three types: Backside-illuminated (BSI), BSI Stacked, and Front-illuminated (FI). BSI sensors enhance light sensitivity by placing the photodiode behind the circuitry, improving image quality in low-light conditions. BSI Stacked sensors further optimize this by adding an additional layer for faster processing and better performance. Front-illuminated sensors, while less efficient in low light, are simpler and cost-effective, making them suitable for many standard applications in consumer electronics.</p></p>
