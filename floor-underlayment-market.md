<p><p>Floor underlayment types include cement board, foam laminate, cork, rubber, plywood, and others. Cement board underlayment provides strong support and moisture resistance, ideal for tile installations. Foam laminate underlayment offers sound absorption and thermal insulation, commonly used with laminate flooring. Cork underlayment is eco-friendly, offering both sound insulation and cushioning. Rubber underlayment is durable and water-resistant, suitable for high-traffic areas. Plywood underlayment delivers stability and helps level uneven floors, while other types encompass a variety of specialized materials for various applications.</p></p>
