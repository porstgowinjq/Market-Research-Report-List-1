<p><p>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer (GFRP) market is categorized primarily into four types: Polyester, Vinyl Ester, Epoxy, and Polyurethane. Polyester is cost-effective and widely used in construction and automotive applications. Vinyl Ester offers enhanced chemical resistance and is favored in marine environments. Epoxy provides superior mechanical properties and durability, making it ideal for aerospace and high-performance applications. Polyurethane combines flexibility with strength, suitable for various industrial uses. Each type serves distinct applications based on their unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
