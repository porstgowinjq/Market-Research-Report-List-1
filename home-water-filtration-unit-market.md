<p><p>The home water filtration unit market comprises various technologies, primarily categorized into RO-based, UV-based, and others. RO (Reverse Osmosis) systems utilize a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities, effectively purifying water from contaminants. UV-based systems employ ultraviolet light to disinfect water, targeting microorganisms without chemical use. Other filtration methods, such as carbon filters and sediment filters, enhance water quality by reducing chlorine, heavy metals, and sediments. Each type caters to varying water quality needs and consumer preferences.</p></p>
