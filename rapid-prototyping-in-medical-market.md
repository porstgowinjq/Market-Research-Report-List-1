<p><strong>The Rapid Prototyping in Medical Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereolithogrphy Apparatus (SLA)</li><li>Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM)</li><li>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)</li><li>Three Dimension Printing (3DP)</li><li>Fused Depostion Modeling (FDM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rapid prototyping in the medical market involves various additive manufacturing technologies that enable the fast creation of models and devices for testing and development. Stereolithography (SLA) uses UV light to solidify liquid resin, producing high-precision parts. Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM) layers adhesive-coated paper, offering cost-effective models. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) fuses powdered materials through laser heat, ideal for complex designs. Three-Dimensional Printing (3DP) applies inks for layer-by-layer construction, while Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) extrudes thermoplastic filaments, known for its accessibility and ease of use.</p></p>
