<p><p>The safety seat market features various types designed for child safety during travel. Rearward-facing baby seats provide optimal protection for infants by supporting their head and neck. Combination seats can transition from rearward to forward-facing as a child grows. Forward-facing child seats safeguard toddlers with harness systems. High-backed booster seats elevate children for seatbelt positioning, while low-backed booster seats offer similar support without a backrest. Booster cushions are simpler, providing a raised seating position for proper seatbelt alignment.</p></p>
