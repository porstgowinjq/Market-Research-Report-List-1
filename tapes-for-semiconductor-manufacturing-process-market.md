<p><p>In the semiconductor manufacturing process, tapes are essential for various applications. "Tape for Backgrinding" is used to secure wafers during the thinning process, ensuring uniformity and reducing breakage risks. It provides stability and protects the wafer's surface. Conversely, "Tape for Dicing" is utilized to hold wafers during the cutting process, facilitating precise division into individual chips without damage. Both tape types play a crucial role in enhancing efficiency and quality in semiconductor production.</p></p>
