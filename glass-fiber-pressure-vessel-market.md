<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Pressure Vessel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PAN-Based</li><li>Pitch-Based</li><li>Viscose-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Fiber Pressure Vessel market is categorized into three main types based on the precursor materials used: PAN-based, pitch-based, and viscose-based. PAN-based vessels, derived from polyacrylonitrile, offer high strength and are commonly used in high-performance applications. Pitch-based vessels, originating from petroleum or coal tar pitch, provide excellent thermal stability and resistance. Viscose-based vessels, made from regenerated cellulose, are less common but offer unique properties suitable for specific applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
