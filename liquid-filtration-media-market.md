<p><p>The liquid filtration media market comprises three main types: mechanical, chemical, and biological medias. Mechanical medias, such as filters and membranes, physically trap particles from fluids. Chemical medias involve substances that remove contaminants through chemical reactions, like activated carbon for adsorption. Biological medias leverage microbial action to break down pollutants, commonly used in wastewater treatment. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring water quality across various industrial, municipal, and environmental applications, catering to diverse filtration needs.</p></p>
