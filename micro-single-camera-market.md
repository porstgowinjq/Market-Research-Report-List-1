<p><strong>The Micro-single Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mirrorless System</li><li>Fixed Mirror System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro-single camera market comprises two main types: mirrorless system cameras and fixed mirror system cameras. Mirrorless system cameras utilize electronic viewfinders and do not have a mirror mechanism, allowing for compact designs, faster autofocus, and advanced video capabilities. In contrast, fixed mirror system cameras, which integrate a fixed mirror, offer a more traditional experience with optical viewfinders while maintaining a smaller footprint compared to DSLRs. Both types cater to photography enthusiasts seeking versatility and high-quality imaging.</p></p>
