<p><p>The knee replacement prosthesis market is divided into three main components: the tibial component, femoral component, and patella component. The tibial component serves as the base that supports weight and allows for movement, while the femoral component replicates the thigh bone's surface for smooth joint motion. The patella component, or knee cap, aids in the joint's stabilization and movement. Each component plays a crucial role in restoring functionality and reducing pain in patients undergoing knee replacement surgery.</p></p>
