<p><p>The heat sterilization equipment market is primarily divided into two types: dry heat sterilization equipment and moist heat sterilization equipment. Dry heat sterilization employs high temperatures over a prolonged period to eliminate microorganisms, making it suitable for heat-stable items such as glassware and metal instruments. In contrast, moist heat sterilization utilizes steam and pressure, effectively killing bacteria and spores, ideal for surgical instruments and laboratory tools. Each type serves distinct applications based on the materials being sterilized and the required efficacy.</p></p>
