<p><p>The motorcycle connected helmet market features two main types: freestanding and embedded helmets. Freestanding helmets are standalone devices equipped with connectivity features, such as Bluetooth, that can be added to existing helmets without altering their structure. Embedded helmets, on the other hand, have integrated technology, such as speakers, microphones, and sensors, designed during the manufacturing process. Both types aim to enhance rider safety and communication, offering features like navigation, music playback, and hands-free calling tailored for motorcycle enthusiasts.</p></p>
