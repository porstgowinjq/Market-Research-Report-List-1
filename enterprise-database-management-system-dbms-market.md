<p><p>Enterprise Database Management Systems (DBMS) are software solutions for managing and manipulating data in large organizations. The market can be divided into two main types: relational databases, which organize data into structured tables with defined relationships, offering strong consistency and transaction support; and nonrelational databases, which include various models like document, key-value, graph, and columnar, providing flexibility and scalability for unstructured or semi-structured data. Both serve different business needs, enabling efficient data handling and decision-making.</p></p>
