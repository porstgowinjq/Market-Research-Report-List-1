<p><ul><li>Outer Cannulas</li><li>Inner Cannulas</li><li>Obturators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC tracheostomy tube market encompasses several key components: outer cannulas, inner cannulas, and obturators. Outer cannulas serve as the main structure, providing stability and airway access. Inner cannulas are removable tubes that facilitate easier cleaning and maintenance, reducing the risk of blockage. Obturators are smooth, tapered devices used to assist in inserting the outer cannula. Together, these components enhance the safety and effectiveness of tracheostomy procedures, catering to diverse patient needs in respiratory care.</p></p>
