<p><p>LED stereo microscopes are categorized into three main types: Monocular, Binocular, and Trinocular stereoscopic microscopes. Monocular stereoscopes feature a single eyepiece, offering a simpler and cost-effective option for basic observation. Binocular stereoscopes have two eyepieces, providing a more comfortable and immersive viewing experience, ideal for detailed work. Trinocular stereoscopes include an extra camera port, allowing for simultaneous viewing and imaging, making them suitable for documentation and analysis in research and educational settings. Each type caters to different user needs and applications.</p></p>
